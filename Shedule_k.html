Morning A typical day for a working mom starts early. She gets up and makes sure everyone in the family has breakfast and lunch ready to go. While doing that, she also gets herself dressed and mentally prepared for work.
Afternoon Then she heads off to work, which can sometimes be stressful because of traffic. But she uses the time to make calls, answer emails, or think about what she needs to do when she gets to work. She has an office timing 10am to 5pm.
Evening Returning from office she is a little bit stressed & tired but after taking a nap she does all the work pending at home. In the evening she wants coffee with a newspaper. Although she is a working woman she reads the newspaper and tries to update herself.
