Mother's Day

Morning The day for a my mother commences early, as she rises to ensure the family is nourished with breakfast and packed lunches before they embark on their day. Additionally, amidst her responsibilities, she devoutly worships and seeks blessings from God.
Afternoon In the afternoon, she prepares lunch for the family and completes her household chores. Afterward, to relax and unwind, she enjoys reading the newspaper or watching TV serials for entertainment. Additionally, she indulges in sewing as a hobby during this time.
Evening In the evening, she heads to the market to gather daily essentials. Upon returning, she begins preparing dinner for the family. While cooking, she takes pleasure in watching her favorite TV serials, adding a touch of enjoyment to her evening routine.
